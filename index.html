My Work

People's Voat

A mobile client for Voat.co

Voat.co is an upcoming reddit-like front page of the internet, but lacks a proper mobile client. Enter Voat.co: a material design inspired Android application. Some of the key libraries used are EventBus, RxAndroid, Picasso and Retrofit. Feel free to test the app yourself on the Play Store.

Geofeelings

A realtime happiness indicator

Geofeelings is a webapplication where people can share their own feelings, connect with others and see how the rest of the world feels. Geofeelings utilizes the MEAN stack to offer realtime connections and serve multiple parallel clients.

VideoVivo Kortrijk

An interactive journey in the city of Kortrijk

An informative video about Kortrijk, a Belgian city targeted towards non-native speakers. Throughout the interactive video you can find, in a simple and intuitive way, information about what to see, where to eat, transport and much more. Video realised in collaboration with the Municipality of Kortrijk and the University of Howest, within the European project SPECIFI

Terra Incognito

A geographical New Media game

Terra Incognita is a geographical knowledge game fully controlled by Leap Motion and visually supported by the Philips Hue.

No time to waste

Mobile game

No time to waste is a mobile game developed with Adobe Edge Animate. The game can be played in a web browser or on Android via Adobe PhoneGab Build. Highscores are kept in a MySQL Database with the help of some PHP code. Have fun.

Euromillions

Banner campaign

I transformed a tv commercial into an online banner campaign. The banners have different formats (large rectangle and leaderboard) and were made in Adobe Edge. Have a look, maybe do a double take.

Sonos

Product site

A dynamic product site for the Sonos Playbar. Behind the scenes, a JSON file is being queried via JQuery. Have a look
